Prima Pro

PRIME PRO for intravenous/intrathecal delivery, is derived from minimally manipulated human umbilical cord tissue with Wharton’s Jelly. This abundant cell source contains high yields of mesenchymal stromal cells and is considered the gold standard in stem therapies. This flowable tissue allograft is intended for homologous use to supplement the recipient’s native tissue and assist in recapitulating the body’s natural regenerative functions.

Prima MSK Shot

PRIME MSK SHOT for intra-articular/subcutaneous/intramuscular delivery is derived from minimally manipulated human umbilical cord tissue with Wharton’s Jelly. This flowable tissue allograft is intended for homologoususe to supplement the recipient’s native tissue and assist in recapitulating the body’s natural regenerative functions. Prime MSK SHOT is not suitable for intravenous delivery.

Invitra CB

INVITRA CB is a minimally manipulated human tissue allograft suspension derived from umbilical cord blood. Cord blood contains a mixed population of cells, including hematopoietic stem cells (HSC) and mesenchymal stem cells (MSC). INVITRA CB has the capacity to self-renew, release growth factors and further develop into more specialized cells. They contribute to tissue homeostasis, anti-inflammatory responses and antioxidant effects. INVITRA CB provides a synergistic effect to offset the naturally occurring processes that typically derive from age and environmental factors.

Invitra TA/AF

INVITRA AT and INVITRA AF are both biological acellular products derived from human amnion. Amniotic tissue is comprised of an extracellular matrix that forms a natural scaffold. It has a series of anti-bacterial, anti-inflammatory, anti-viral properties as well as low immunogenicity. Amniotic fluid is rich in growth factors and cytokines including growth factor (EGF), transforming growth factor alpha (TGF alpha), transforming growth factor beta-1, and insulin-like growth factor 1. Importantly, it contains factors related to the innate immune systemincluding antimicrobials effective against bacteria, fungi, protozoa, and viruses.

Regen Creme

Regen Crème is a scientifically formulated, nutrient and protein-rich topical crème derived from human umbilical cord tissue. With an abundant supply of growth factors, cytokines, and exosomes, Regen Crème’s advanced healing properties are attributed to the anti-inflamatory and immune modulating functions of mesenchymal stem cells found in cord tissue. Through rapid absorption technology, highly active ingredients readily pass through dermal layers and lipid membranes to transform aged tissues or improve damaged cells.
